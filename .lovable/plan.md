# Personal Portfolio & Career Website

## Overview

A professional multi-page portfolio website for a bioengineering master's student with product management and R&D experience, targeting project management roles. Features a **blue & green & bright** color scheme with a polished, corporate aesthetic.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home / Hero Page

- Strong headline with your name, title, and a brief tagline (e.g., "Bioengineering × Product Management")
- Professional photo/avatar area
- Quick navigation cards linking to other sections
- Call-to-action buttons: "View Projects" and "Download Resume"

### 2. About Me

- Your career story — from a bachlore degree in biomedical engineering to R&D engineering to product management to your master's in bioengineering
- Personal story - moved to the U.S with my husband to putsue my masters in bioengineering focusing on medical device development.
- My hobbies
- Key skills and competencies (visual skill tags or cards)
- Education and experience timeline
- Interests and what drives you

### 3. Experience

- Visual timeline of your work experience (medical device startup PM role, R&D at large company, etc.)
- Each entry with role, company, dates, and key accomplishments
- Clean, scannable layout

### 4. Projects

- **GitHub Integration**: Auto-fetch your public repositories using the GitHub API, displaying repo name, description, language, and stars
- **Featured Projects**: Ability to pin/highlight key projects with richer descriptions and images
- Filter/sort by technology or category

### 5. Blog

- Dynamic blog section powered by **Lovable Cloud** (Supabase backend)
- Create, edit, and publish posts through a simple admin interface
- Blog listing page with titles, dates, and previews
- Individual blog post pages with full content

### 6. Resume / CV

- Downloadable PDF resume button
- Optional: inline resume preview on the page

### 7. Hire Me

- Why I'm looking for a job (graduating May 2026, seeking full-time)
- What I'm looking for (product engineering in medtech)
- What I bring (3 key strengths with examples)
- How to reach me
- Why to hire me

### 8. Contact

- Contact form (name, email, message) — submissions stored via Lovable Cloud
- Links to LinkedIn, GitHub, and email
- Clean footer with social links across all pages

---

## Navigation

- Top navigation bar with links: Home, About, Experience, Projects, Blog, Contact
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu
- Smooth, professional transitions between pages

## Design

- **Bright background** with **Bright Accent/green accents** for a medical/biotech feel
- Clean typography, generous whitespace
- Subtle animations on scroll for timeline and project cards
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile

## Backend (Lovable Cloud)

- Database tables for blog posts and contact form submissions
- Simple admin area for managing blog content
- GitHub API integration for fetching repositories (no auth needed for public repos)
